About EduLearn

Our story, mission, and the team behind the platform.

Our Mission

EduLearn was founded in 2020 with a single goal: make world-class education accessible to every learner in Africa and beyond. We partner with industry experts and universities to deliver practical, job-ready skills through engaging online courses.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital learning platform in Africa, empowering 1 million learners by 2030 to unlock economic opportunity through education.
